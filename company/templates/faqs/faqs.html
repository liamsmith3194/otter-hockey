FAQ's

Orders

To make a purchase from the site, simply browse through our range of products. When you see something that you would like to purchase, select the appropriate size and quantity then click the 'Add to Basket' button. This will store the item until checkout for you to add to if wanted. When you have added all the items you wish to purchase to your basket click on the trolley icon button at the top right of the page. You should then verify that the items in your basket are correct. If you're ready to buy you will need to enter your billing and delivery details, and finally your payment details. Once this process is complete and successful you will receive an email confirming the details of your order.

If you need to cancel your order before it has been posted please contact Customer Services using our contact form. Providing your order hasn't been dispatched we should be able to cancel and give you a full refund.

Delivery

Delivery charges for orders with a value under £100.00 is £5.00. These apply for the UK mainland only. Delivery charges vary outside of the UK.

The majority of the orders are despatched within 3 - 5 days and in-case we don't have stock at hand then we will inform you the timescales involved.

We use Business post, Fedex, DHL, DX, MyHermes, DPD.

If no one is at the delivery address when our carrier tries to deliver your order, the carrier will leave notification of an attempted delivery. The note will specify if you need to contact them to arrange an alternative delivery date, collect the parcel in person or if they will re-attempt delivery the following day.

Returns

You can return it back to us in an unused condition within 14 days. We will be happy to refund your money, excluding postage.

In the unlikely event that you received an incorrect or damaged item in your order, please contact Customer Services using our contact form and someone will be in contact ASAP.
